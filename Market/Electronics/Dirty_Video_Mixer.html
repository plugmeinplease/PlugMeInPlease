Circuit Bending

Dirty Video Mixer


A true Classic. The dirty video mixer is an extremely simple beginner circuit that features 2 in 1 out mixing between two sources. This creates messy scrolling disortion when blending between the two. There are also two toggle switches to turn on and off each input.
