Patch Notes

Here's some wack-o patching methods i've come up with.

  • Summed modulation:

    By using a sum you can create extremely complex envelopes. In this example, ill start by using a mult on Env 1 and Env 2. This isn't necessary, but I like to get incresingly complex modulation patchways. Env 1 modulates Filter cutoff, while Env 2 modulates the "harmonics" function on the Basimilis Iteritas Alter. I then take my 2nd output of each and feed Env 1 and Env 2 into a sum, then spew that into the "morph" function of the BIA. What you get is related modulation, but extremely strange envelope shaping with positive and negative addition of voltages.

  • Noisy Dithering:

    I haven't found a lot of literature about this but by injecting a noise source -> Attenuator -> the same VCA signal as your audio, you can create a sort of dithering effect. betlow 20% Noise in the signal, you can create quite pleasent sounds, and from the 25% and up your rack will howl with all sorts of digital aliasing and FM crazyness.
